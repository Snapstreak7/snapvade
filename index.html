Privacy Policy

Welcome to SNAPSTREAK. Your privacy is critically important to us.

1. Information Collection: This tool is designed for educational and recovery purposes. We do not store, log, or distribute any usernames or target profiles entered into our system.

2. Security: Any data fetched or analyzed is temporarily processed in memory and immediately discarded after your session ends.

3. Third-Party Services: We may use third-party security services (like reCAPTCHA) to prevent bot abuse. These services have their own privacy policies.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this tool safe to use?

Yes, the tool is 100% safe and anonymous. The target user will not be notified of the analysis.

Do I need the user's password?

No. SNAPSTREAK operates by exploiting publicly available cache data and network endpoints, meaning no passwords are required.

Why do I need to complete human verification?

Due to the high volume of automated bots trying to scrape our data, human verification is temporarily required to protect our servers and keep the tool free for real users.
